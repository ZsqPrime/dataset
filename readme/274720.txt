Blog.txt
========

A simple blogging tool.

Setup
------

Edit config.php as appropriate.

Then copy the following files and folders into your server:

 - css/
 - img/
 - js/
 - includes/
 - config.php
 - index.php
 - rss.php

And visit your site.



Project Description:
Plaintext to web-based blog.
