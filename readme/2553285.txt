Project Description:
Background service for Android facilitating access to sensors and networking from browser-based apps

README:
ubihelper - an android background service to provide access to sensors 

and maybe networking from AppFurnace and similar PhoneGap browser-style applications

i.e. over local HTTP.



Chris Greenhalgh, The University of Nottingham, 2012
