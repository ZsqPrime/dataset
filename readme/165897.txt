Tired of clicking on that sweet, shortened link in Twitter (t.co) or the full-on url (twitter.com) and getting the dreaded "Sorry, but we couldn’t find that link!" page becuase of some lame punctuation at the end?

Cry no more! This simple Chrome extension will removed those unwanted ")" and "." and "," from the end of your twitter links! Breathe easy and live your life to the fullest!
