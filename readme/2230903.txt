LL Compiler is a small Little Lisp Language compiler. Developed by Danil Tumaykin and Igor Dultsev at Intel WS2012 at NSU-Intel Lab.

Further documentation is available at http://www.i-lab.nsu.ru/wiki/index.php/LL_Compiler.
